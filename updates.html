NoteCube Updates

v.2.0 | 6/23/2024

  • The second major version of NoteCube got released! We released a lot of changes, updates, new features, improvements, and more!
  • NoteCube got a brand new redesign and revamp so it's easier than ever to use and navigate the app, looks cleaner, more modern, and comfortable. We also improved the designs on multiple elements for an enhanced functionality performance and more responsive and accessible. We also rolled out new layouts and positionings of the elements for you to focus on what's important and not get distracted.
  • We created new pages such as this Updates page, updated a lot of content, images, descriptions, and more throughout the app's pages. We also reorganized and deleted some unneccesary files and elements for a faster experience on NoteCube!
  • We fixed some notes and appearance bugs, and optimized our code for the best and the fastest user experience on NoteCube! We also organized content and elements on each page to look more unified and clean.

v.1.5 | 6/1/2024

  • We released the new Link Clipboard feature, where you can paste many websites' URL addresses to NoteCube and access them directly from here! This makes visiting your favorite websites easier and faster than ever, with other link managing features like add link, delete link, and more!
  • Updated some appearance and styling on each page and some elements to make your experience on NoteCube better.

v.1.0 | 5/7/2024

  • Initial release of the app with a brand new layout, user interface, user experience, functionalities, pages, content, and more.
  • We released the Notes page to take writing notes to another level with useful and powerful functionalities such as Save, New Note, change font size, Delete, and more.
  • We planned a lot of upcoming features, changes, and updates that'll make NoteCube better than ever!
